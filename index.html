March ​Early Release/No School Days/Conferences Coming Up:
Monday, March 26th  - Early Release
Thursday, March 29th  - Early Release
**For Early Release Days please remember that all students will be released at 12:50 p.m. Students attending the Technology Center will attend as if a regular scheduled day and will return back to the school at 3:00 p.m.
Friday, March 30th - No School

Conferences- March 26th and 29th, K-12, all conferences start at 1:15 pm and go through 7:00 pm. Elementary conferences are scheduled by appointment. Middle school and high school conferences will be held in teacher classrooms. Report cards will be available for pickup upon check in at the middle/high school office.

​School District Announcement
As of Wednesday, February 28, 2018 Mr. Phil McAndrew will be stepping down as MS/HS Principal, Virtual Academy and Director of Transportation.  In this transition Mr. Marc Kline, long-time teacher and coach in the district will be stepping in as the interim MS/HS Principal/Director of Transportation to finish the 2017/18 school year.
